Blood Donation Management System

About Us

The Blood Donation Management System is a simple platform designed to connect blood donors with patients in need. Our aim is to make it easier for hospitals and individuals to find donors quickly in emergency situations.

Features include donor registration, searching donors by blood group and city, viewing donor lists, and submitting blood requests.

This project is developed as a mini-project for educational purposes and to promote the importance of voluntary blood donation.
